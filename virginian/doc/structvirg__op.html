Detailed Description

Opcode, including arguments.

This struct is used to store all the attributes of an opcode, including the 3 integer arguments and the fourth variable-type argument, in addition to the integer opcode corresponding to an op in the virg_ops enum. The 4 arguments may be option, and their use is entirely dependent on the current opcode being executed.
